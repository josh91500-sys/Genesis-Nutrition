Privacy Policy Last updated: March 31, 2026 Genesis Nutrition ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Genesis Nutrition mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information. Information We Collect Genesis Nutrition stores all food log data locally on your device. We do not collect or store personal information on our servers. When you use the AI food lookup feature, your food descriptions are sent to Anthropic's API to generate nutritional estimates. No personal identifying information is included in these requests. How We Use Information Food and nutrition data you enter is stored only on your device using local storage. This data is never transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties. Third Party Services We use Anthropic's Claude API to power AI food lookup and label scanning features. Food descriptions you enter may be processed by Anthropic. Please review Anthropic's Privacy Policy for more information. Device Permissions The app may request access to your device camera to scan nutrition labels. Photos are processed locally and are not stored or uploaded to any server. Children's Privacy Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Data Security All your nutrition data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit or store your personal health data on any external servers. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
